Mentor Information

Welcome to the mentor's section of this site. As a mentor, your role is to help and support students as they learn web development through this course. You do not need to be a web development expert, but you should be willing to assist students with any questions they may have and encourage them to keep going. Feel free to challenge them to think critically about what they are learning and how they can apply it. Remember, your enthusiasm and support can make a big difference in a student's learning journey. Thank you for being a mentor.
