Privacy Policy

Introduction

#

Welcome to Web Witchcraft and Wizardry. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring that your personal information is handled in a safe and responsible manner. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you visit our website.

Information We Collect

This website does not use cookies, tracking scripts, or analytics tools to collect information about your browsing behavior. We do not automatically collect any personal data when you visit our website. However, we do use your browser's local storage to save your personal preferences (such as avatar and theme selections) locally on your device for your convenience.

Feedback Form Data

The only personal data we collect is voluntarily provided by you through our feedback form. When you submit feedback, we may collect the following information:

  • Your name (optional)
  • Your email address (optional)
  • Your role (e.g., student, mentor, parent, teacher)
  • Demographic information (age range, gender - optional)
  • Technical information about your computer and browser setup
  • Your location type (e.g., home, school, library)
  • Your feedback and suggestions about the course

Submission of feedback is entirely voluntary. You may choose not to provide personal information, though this may limit our ability to respond to specific feedback.

Local Storage

This website uses your browser's local storage to save your personal preferences and enhance your user experience. Local storage is a feature built into your web browser that allows websites to store small amounts of data on your device. This data remains on your device and is not transmitted to our servers or any third parties.

We use local storage to remember your choices, including:

  • Your selected avatar (witch or wizard character)
  • Your preferred colour theme (light, dark, subdued, or vibrant)
  • Other user interface preferences

These preferences are stored locally on your device to provide a personalised experience when you return to the website. You can clear this data at any time by clearing your browser's local storage or using your browser's privacy settings. No personal or identifiable information is stored in local storage.

How We Use Your Information

Any information collected through the feedback form is used solely for the following purposes:

  • To improve the content, structure, and usability of our website
  • To understand who is using our educational resources
  • To identify technical issues and improve accessibility
  • To develop better learning materials based on user feedback
  • To respond to your specific feedback or questions (if you have provided contact information)

Data Storage and Security

We take appropriate security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorised access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, please note that no method of electronic storage is 100% secure.

Feedback data is stored securely and is accessible only to authorized personnel who need it to improve the website and respond to user feedback.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We do not share your data with any external organizations, advertisers, or marketing companies.

Your feedback data may be reviewed by members of the Web Witchcraft and Wizardry development team solely for the purpose of improving the educational content and user experience.

Data Retention

We retain feedback data only for as long as necessary to fulfil the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy. Feedback may be retained to track improvements over time and ensure the continuous enhancement of our educational resources.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Request deletion of your personal data
  • Withdraw consent for data processing at any time
  • Object to the processing of your personal data

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the information provided below.

Children's Privacy

This website is designed for educational purposes and may be used by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children without parental consent. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided personal information through our feedback form without your consent, please contact us immediately, and we will delete such information.

We strongly encourage parents, teachers, and mentors to supervise children's online activities and guide them when submitting feedback.

Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to external websites for educational or reference purposes. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of these third-party sites. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of any external websites you visit.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. The "Last Updated" date at the top of this page will indicate when the most recent changes were made. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically.
